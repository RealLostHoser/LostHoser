1999

My second-favourite country in the world. Growing up, my Dad took my brother and I, and our trusty minivan on countless road trips through the States. From New York to California, Texas to Maine, I've seen it all (with the exception of Alaska, Hawaii, Oregon, Washington State). Most recently I strapped a tent to my Harley and rode through New England in 2023. Shoot, I went to Vegas for my parents' wedding while I was still in the womb. I love this place and its people. I'll take my green card now.
