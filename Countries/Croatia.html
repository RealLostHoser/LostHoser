The land of my ancestors. My father spent the better part of his childhood in Zagreb, and grew into a lady-killer guiding tours in Istria. I got the chance to visit his childhood home from the outside (a run-down apartment). I video-called him while I was there. He showed me the spots downtown he frequented in real time. I'm glad we got to share that moment. 
He passed away in December 2024.
